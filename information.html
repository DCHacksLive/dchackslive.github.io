Important Information


Questions/concerns: You can reach the DCHacks Team at (571)-830-2303. Please only call us if the issue is urgent.

1. (WIFI) SSID: CGUEST, Password: c@rpeted

2. Access at Cvent: The only access in and out of Cvent's office is through Tower 1 to the 7th floor. If hackers exit at any other location, they will be taken to the lobby. Please ONLY enter and exit through Tower 1. If you go through the exit doors on Tower 2, you will be locked out. DO NOT PROP DOORS. If you see attendees doing this, close the doors. Please note that you may not leave during the hackathon without permission from the DCHacks Team. Your parents will be notified if this happens.

3. No printing: Attendees will not be able to print to any of Cvent's printers. (This is enforced via the networking changes made in the office.)

4. No video conferencing: Attendees will not be able to use conference room video conferencing. (This is enforced via the networking changes made in the office.)

5. No phones: Attendees are not to use Cvent's phones. If you see anyone using the phones, please ask politely who they are speaking to and use discretion in deciding whether to allow the call to continue or ask the attendee to hang up.

6. No Cvent PCs: Attendees are not to use Cvent's PCs; this includes conference room PCs and (especially) end-user workstations.

7. Attendees stay put: Per DCHacks rules, once checked-in, all attendees must stay onsite for the duration of the event. That means attendees MAY NOT leave the office until the event concludes.

8. Restroom locations: Restrooms may be ONLY accessed through Tower 1.

9. Emergency info:
  • If you have an emergency, please find a DCHacks team member located on the 7th floor in the Galileo conference room. If this is a medical emergency, dial 911.
  • Fire: Head to the nearest exit. Evacuate through the stairs next to the elevator banks in Towers 1 and 2. Maps of the emergency exits and muster zones are posted on each floor in the copy room.
10. Sleeping:
  • Sleeping area for women: Located in Tower 2 on Floors 6 and 7. For more information, please see a DCHacks team member or Cvent representative.
  • Sleeping area for men: Located in Tower 1 on Floor 7 in the Plato conference room. For more information, please see a DCHacks team member or Cvent representative.
