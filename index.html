Welcome to DCHacks Live!

To the left is a sidebar to navigate between the various pages. We'll making anouncments through Twitter, so make sure to follow @DCHacksOfficial! If you don't have a Twitter, we have embedded our profile here.

  • The venue map on the sidebar will help you find where the workshops, games, sponsors, and food are!
  • If you are new to programming or have never been to a hackathon before, don't worry! We suggest that you look at the coding tracks on the left. Each track has its own list of tutorials to help you learn more about that track!
  • If you are looking for a fun break during the hackathon, we also have some awesome events during the night! Winners will receive special prizes. Space is limited, so make sure to come early!
  • We also have a multitude of workshops running throughout the event. Make sure to check them out and learn something new!
  • If at any time you have a question or require a mentor, click the "Request a mentor" button at the top right, and a mentor will come to you.
